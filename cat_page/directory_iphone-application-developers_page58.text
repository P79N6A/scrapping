<h2>What to Know About iPhone App Development Companies</h2>
<p> </p>
<p>As of March 2017, Apple’s App Store had <a href="https://www.statista.com/statistics/276623/number-of-apps-available-in-leading-app-stores/" target="_blank">2.2 million apps</a>. From March 2017 - May 2017, over <a href="https://9to5mac.com/2017/08/09/us-iphone-sales-ios-market-share-kantar/" target="_blank">one-third of all smartphones</a> sold in the United States were iPhones, up nearly 5% from the year prior. In Clutch’s recent survey on <a href="https://clutch.co/app-developers/resources/cost-build-mobile-app-survey" target="_blank">app development cost</a>, 75% of the 102 app development companies surveyed had built an iOS application in the past year. Clearly, there is demand for iPhones, and thus iOS developers. Many businesses seeking to build an app may currently be searching specifically for an iPhone app development company.</p>
<p>If you are searching for iOS developers, then you should understand two aspects of iPhone app development before moving forward:</p>
<ul><li>You should clearly understand the differences between hybrid and native applications.</li>
<p></p>
<li>You should be aware of the cost of iPhone app development companies.</li>
</ul><p>Read below to better understand these basics regarding iPhone app development companies.</p>
<p> </p>
<h3>Understand the Difference Between Hybrid and Native Applications</h3>
<p>There are two main types of apps: hybrid apps and native apps. What is the difference between a hybrid app and a native app?</p>
<h4> </h4>
<h4>Hybrid Apps Work Across iOS, Android, and Windows Apps</h4>
<p>Hybrid apps work across multiple operating systems. Therefore, if you are building an app that works on iOS, Android and Windows, you are building a hybrid app. Hybrid apps are essentially web browsers that are built within a thin native application framework. Hybrid apps are cheaper and faster to build. However, hybrid apps are limited in their capabilities, and tend to be slower. They offer less streamlined UX and cannot support intricate graphics.</p>
<h4> </h4>
<h4>iOS App Developers Build Native Apps</h4>
<p>If you are looking to build an app specifically for iPhones, you are building a native application.</p>
<p>Native applications run on a specific operating system - in iPhone’s case, iOS. If you build a native iPhone app, it won’t run on Android phones. Native applications are written in the intended operating system’s specific language. This would likely be Swift for iPhones, Java for Android phones, and C# for Windows phones.</p>
<p>Native apps run faster and better on their specific operating system. They have less glitches and can offer more complex functionality than hybrid apps. The downside of native apps is, if you want to target a wide audience, you’ll likely need to build both a native iOS and native Android application. Native apps are also more expensive.</p>
<p>iPhone app development companies may be able to build both native and hybrid apps, but if you’re specifically looking to target iPhone users, than a native app is the best choice.</p>
<p> </p>
<h3>What Affects The Cost of iPhone App Development Companies</h3>
<p>As mentioned above, native applications tend to cost more. However, just how expensive is it to build an iOS app while working with an iPhone app development company?</p>
<p>Overall, Clutch found in its <a href="https://clutch.co/app-developers/resources/cost-build-mobile-app-survey" target="_blank">app development cost survey</a> that 44% of app development companies reported that their last launched app cost $50,000 or less. Another 23% said it cost $50,000 - $100,000. Then, 29% reported that their last launched app cost more than $100,000. Of those 29%, 4 companies built apps costing more than $1,000,000.</p>
<p>While these numbers consider both iOS, Android, and hybrid app development, iOS development will likely fall on the higher end of this pay scale. Unfortunately, knowing exactly how much it costs to work with an iPhone app development company is complicated.</p>
<p>There are two factors that will significantly impact the final price tag:</p>
<ol><li>The intricacy of your app development project</li>
<p></p>
<li>The location of your iPhone app development company</li>
</ol><p>A complicated app will obviously cost more money. If you expect an app with a level of functionality on par with Uber, then you should be saving up a very large chunk of money. However, you likely don’t need every function possible to develop a successful iOS app. A good iPhone app development company will work with you to understand which features are tailored well for your audience and which can be scrapped.</p>
<p>The location of your iPhone app development company will also strongly impact the final price tag. Offshore development is a popular option for many U.S. businesses looking into building an app for cheaper. Hotspots for offshore app development include:</p>
<ul><li>India</li>
<p></p>
<li>Eastern Europe</li>
<p></p>
<li>Latin America</li>
</ul><p>However, you should strongly consider the unique obstacles associated with offshore development before making a decision on the location of your iPhone app development company. Be sure to set up a clear plan of communication with an offshore development company to work around the geographical distance and time zone differences.</p>
<p> </p>
<h3>Seek Out iOS App Developers with Caution and Commitment</h3>
<p>Making the decision to build an iOS application is a large one for a business. Any app is a big investment, both in time and money, and native iOS applications require significant commitment. However, reaching your audience with a well-built and targeted iOS application can significantly boost your business. Use Clutch’s reviews to shop for the best iPhone app development company for your needs.</p>
